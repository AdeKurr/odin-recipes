Omelette

Description

Who doesn't love a plate of omelette? An easy-made food for you who have short amount of time to have breakfast. It's simple, easy, and cheap. 5 minutes is all you need to have. How do we make it? Here we go!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Chillis
  • Onions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heaten the cooking oil.
  2. Crack the eggs on a bowl.
  3. Slices some onions and chillis.
  4. Pour the slices into the eggs.
  5. Add salt.
  6. Mix them all together.
  7. Pour the mixture into the hot pan.
  8. Keep the mixture in the shape.
  9. Flip the egg if it's already harder.
  10. Put the egg on the plate.
  11. The omelette is ready to be served!
