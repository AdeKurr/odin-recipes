Instant Noodles

Description

Instant noodles are the most convenient food ever existed in the whole plane of the world. The noodles is made from eggs and flour, while the seasonings are from salt and spices. Instant noodles has been around for ages and everyone still love it till today. It's all thanks to how convenient it is.

Ingredients

  • The whole package of instant-noodles
  • Water
  • Pan

Steps

  1. Heaten the water on the pan.
  2. After it boils, put the instant noodles on the boiling water.
  3. Open the seasonings and pour them into a plate
  4. Once the noodles softens, put it on the plate
  5. Mix it all together.
  6. The instant noodles is ready to be served.
