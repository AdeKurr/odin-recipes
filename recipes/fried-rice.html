Fried Rice

Description

Cook a delicious fried rice for your family and friends! a plate of fried rice containing chillis, eggs, shrimps, and obviously, rice! Interested? Follow along!

Ingredients

  • A plate of rice
  • Eggs
  • Chillis
  • Onions
  • Shallot
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Any additions

Steps

  1. Heaten the cooking oil on the pan.
  2. Crack an egg and put it on the pan.
  3. Scramble the egg
  4. While waiting for the egg to cook, slice the onions, shallots, and the chillis.
  5. Pour the seasonings into the pan, mix it with the egg.
  6. Pour the rice and mix it along the egg and the seasonings.
  7. Pour enough soy sauce.
  8. Mix all until everything is mixed well.
  9. Pour all on the plate.
  10. Your favorite fried rice is ready.
